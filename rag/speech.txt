Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is an advanced AI architecture combining retrieval-based and generation-based methods. It involves three key components: the retriever, which fetches relevant information from a large dataset; the generator, typically a sophisticated language model like GPT-4, which creates coherent responses; and the fusion module, which integrates these elements seamlessly. RAG enhances applications in customer support, knowledge management, content creation, and research by providing accurate, contextually relevant information efficiently. As RAG evolves, it promises to significantly impact various fields, driving innovation and improving decision-making processes.
